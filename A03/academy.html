About the academy

A student first centered approach to teaching

Neha founded the academy in early 2005. Here she inspires all her students to strive for perfection and the individual focused training brings out the best in them. She is very popular in the Nashua area for her authentic style of teaching and uncompromising discipline that helps her students reach the highest standards in dance.

While she specializes in teaching Bharatanatyam and Mohiniattam she is very knowledgeable and adept with the Folk dances of India. She also teaches the popular Bollywood style dances.
